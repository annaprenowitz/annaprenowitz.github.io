Amaranta's Death

July, 2024

Written, dir. Anna Prenowitz

Videography and editing by Anna Prenowitz

Starring Anna Prenowitz

A short film loosely based on one short page in Gabriel García Márquez's epic, One Hundred Years of Solitude. Amaranta makes preparations after having received the news of her imminent death. Anna shot this film by herself in her grandfather's house.
